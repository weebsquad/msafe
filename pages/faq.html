What is metalsafe?

metalsafe is an easy to use, open source and completely free file upload service. We accept your files, photos, documents, anything, and give you back a shareable link for you to send to others.

Encoding?

Encoding is a just a simple ID encoding applied to the image name and extension, encoding is done server-side, and parsed when used on an URL. You can find more info about it here
